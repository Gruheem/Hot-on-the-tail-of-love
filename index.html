Helping Dogs find their Forever Families...

Established in 2001, our shelter began as a small, heartfelt effort by Barry and Sheila—two lifelong dog lovers—who believed every dog deserves a safe, loving home. What started as a passion project has since grown into a trusted local hub for rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption.

Who We Are

Founded in 2001 by Barry and Sheila, our shelter began with one goal: to give dogs a second chance. Since then, we’ve grown into a trusted, community-driven place where dogs find safety, love, and forever homes.

We’re more than a shelter—we’re a place where lasting bonds form between people and pets. As we grow, we’re focused on outreach, education, and building stronger community ties. Thanks for being part of the journey.

Adoption has Never been Easier!

Adopting a dog has never been easier. With simple online listings, helpful staff, and a quick, caring process, finding your perfect furry friend is just a few steps away. Whether you're a seasoned pet owner or a first-timer, your new best friend could be waiting for you today.

Adoption in 4 Easy Steps

1. Fill Out Our Online Form

Please share preferences with us as this helps us match you with the right dog!

2. Meet Your Match

We’ll be in touch to arrange a visit with dogs that suit your home, family, and energy level.

3. Home Check & Approval

A quick, friendly home check ensures it’s a safe, happy environment for your future furry friend.

4. Take Them Home

Once approved, just like that, your new best friend comes home!
