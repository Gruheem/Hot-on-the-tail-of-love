Who We Are

Founded in 2001 by Barry and Sheila, our dog adoption shelter began as a simple mission: to give every dog the second chance they deserve. What started as a small rescue effort has grown into a trusted, community-focused shelter where dogs find safety, love, and forever homes. With compassion at our core, we’ve connected countless dogs with families ready to welcome them.

We're more than a shelter—we’re a place where meaningful bonds are formed between people and pets. As we grow, we’re committed to expanding our impact through education, outreach, and deeper community partnerships. Whether you're here to adopt, volunteer, or support in another way, we’re glad you’re part of the journey.
